PERFORMANCE

Page Speed Optimization

Mobile PageSpeed score improved from 48 to 92 with image, JS, and edge cache refactor.

Challenge

A SaaS marketing site scored 48 on Google PageSpeed (mobile). High bounce rates on mobile (71%) and poor Core Web Vitals were hurting both UX and paid search Quality Scores.

Solution

  • Converted all images to WebP/AVIF with responsive srcset and lazy-loading
  • Eliminated 3 render-blocking JS bundles via code-splitting and deferred loads
  • Deployed edge caching (Cloudflare) with stale-while-revalidate strategy
  • Removed 4 unused third-party scripts (chat widget, legacy analytics)
  • Inlined critical CSS, deferred non-critical stylesheets

CONVERSION

Ecommerce Conversion Growth

Conversion rate increased from 2.3% to 4.1% through funnel redesign and A/B testing.

Challenge

A DTC brand was driving solid traffic (120k visits/month) but converting at 2.3% — well below the 3.5% industry average. Cart abandonment sat at 79% and checkout drop-off was severe on mobile.

Solution

  • Full funnel audit: heatmaps, session recordings, and exit-survey data
  • Redesigned PDP with social proof, urgency signals, and clearer CTAs
  • Simplified checkout from 5 steps → 2 steps (guest checkout enabled)
  • A/B tested 6 variations of the add-to-cart flow over 10 days
  • Abandoned cart email sequence (3-touch) recovering 12% of drop-offs
